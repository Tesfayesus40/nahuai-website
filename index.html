The process

ሂደቱ

የግኝት ጥሪ

Knowledge base build

የዕውቀት መሰረት ግንባታ

You send us your documents, FAQs, and policies. We turn them into the bot's Amharic knowledge base.

ግንባታ እና ሙከራ

We build, test in Amharic, and refine until the bot responds correctly to 95%+ of questions.
