Privacy Policy

Nahu AI Translation Services  ·  Last updated: June 2026  ·  Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada

This policy explains how Nahu AI Translation Services collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our document translation service.

1. Information we collect

When you use our translation service, we collect the following:

  • Contact information: Your name and email address, provided at checkout or when contacting us.
  • Payment information: Payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Documents you submit: Official documents you send for translation, which may include birth certificates, marriage certificates, academic credentials, employment letters, and similar personal documents.
  • Communication records: Emails and messages you exchange with us regarding your order.

2. How we use your information

  • To complete and deliver your translation order
  • To communicate with you about your order status
  • To correct any errors in your translation
  • To maintain order records for our business accounting

We do not use your personal information for marketing purposes without your consent.

3. Who has access to your documents

Your documents are handled only by:

  • Nahu AI staff — Tesfayesus Yirdaw (Director) who manages your order
  • Your assigned translator reviewer — a native Amharic speaker under a confidentiality agreement

We never share, sell, or distribute your documents or personal information to any third party.

4. Data retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Client documents (birth certificates, diplomas, etc.): Permanently deleted 30 days after your translation is delivered
  • Order records (name, email, document type, amount): Retained for 7 years for accounting and tax purposes as required by Canadian law
  • Payment records: Retained by Stripe according to their policy

5. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Order data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database with encryption at rest
  • We recommend using secure file transfer methods for sensitive documents — ask us about our secure upload option
  • Translator reviewers are bound by a confidentiality agreement

No transmission over the internet is 100% secure. By using our service you acknowledge this risk.

6. Your rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent to our use of your information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tesfa@nahuai.app.

7. Cookies

Our website (nahuai.app) does not use tracking cookies. Stripe's checkout page may use cookies as described in Stripe's privacy policy.
