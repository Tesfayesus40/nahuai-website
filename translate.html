This depends entirely on your institution's specific requirements. IRCC generally accepts translations accompanied by a translator's attestation statement, but requirements can vary by application type and processing office. We strongly recommend verifying your institution's exact requirements before ordering. If you are unsure, contact us first and we will advise you honestly.
Every translation includes: (1) the translated document as a Word file, (2) a signed translator attestation statement on Nahu AI letterhead as a PDF. The attestation states that the translator is a native Amharic speaker and that the translation is accurate to the best of their knowledge.
The +$25 notarization option means a Saskatoon notary public witnesses and notarizes the translator's signature on the attestation document. Important: a notary verifies the identity of the signer and witnesses the signature — they do not verify the accuracy of the translation. Some institutions require a notarized signature in addition to a translation. Check your institution's requirements before adding this option.
After payment you will receive a confirmation email. Reply to that email with your document attached as a PDF or clear photo. A scanned copy is sufficient as long as all text is legible. For sensitive documents we recommend using a secure file sharing method — ask us about our secure upload option.
Your documents are handled only by Nahu AI and your assigned translator reviewer. We never share client documents with third parties. Documents are permanently deleted from our systems 30 days after delivery. We maintain a strict confidentiality agreement with all translators.
We will correct any factual errors in names, dates, or document numbers at no charge. Email us within 7 days of delivery. Our maximum liability is limited to a full refund of the amount paid.
